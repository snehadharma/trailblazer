Ropest is a decorative script typeface, created by Måns Grebäck during 2018.
It keeps the same quality as any cursive font, while also being a brave display typeface.

The family comes in two styles; one primary that has the real rope appearance; one Black or solid, to be used by itself or as a background to the rope font. 

It contains ligatures, and has support for all Latin based European languages.
